Our Story

More Than A Gym.
A Movement.

Zelha Spin & Fitness was born from a simple but powerful dream — to build more than a gym. We set out to create a home for fitness right here in Juja; a place where every rep, every spin, and every drop of sweat brings you closer to the best version of yourself.

Our vision doesn't stop at these walls. We're growing into a renowned health club — one that reaches beyond Juja and stands for something bigger. We're building a family. A community of people who choose to show up, push limits, and live with purpose.

Because at Zelha, fitness isn't just something you do. It's something you belong to.
